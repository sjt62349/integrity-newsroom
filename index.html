Privacy Policy

The Integrity Newsroom app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It does not track users, does not use analytics, and does not transmit any information to external servers.

The app simply displays publicly available news content and does not require any login or personal information.

Data Collection

Integrity Newsroom collects no data of any kind. No personal information, usage data, device identifiers, or analytics are gathered.
